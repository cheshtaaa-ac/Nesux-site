About NexusX

💻 Who We Are

NexusX is the official technical club of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Bharati Vidyapeeth's College of Engineering, New Delhi. Founded by students, for students — we believe the best learning happens outside the classroom.

🎯 Our Mission

To create a vibrant tech community where students explore, experiment, and excel. We bridge the gap between academics and industry through hands-on projects, competitive coding, and collaborations with top tech firms.

⚡ What We Do

Weekly coding sessions, monthly hackathons, guest lectures from industry professionals, open-source contributions, competitive programming contests, and placement preparation workshops.

🏆 Why Join Us

Build your portfolio, grow your network, win competitions, and get placement-ready. Our alumni are at Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Flipkart, and top startups across India and the world.
